Marc Zinaman / Making Queer History

Marc Zinaman is a contributing writer to the website Making Queer History, where he documents queer historical figures from underrepresented countries. These have included:

  1. François Benga

  2. Alla Nazimova

  3. Francisco Moncion

  4. "Panama" Al Brown

  5. Armen Ohanian
