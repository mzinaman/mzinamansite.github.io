Marc Zinaman / NYC LGBT Historic Sites Project

Marc Zinaman has been a contributing writer for the NYC LGBT Historic Sites Project, where he has researched and written about the long lost spaces listed below, as well as interviewed significant individuals who were involved with them:

  • Club 57
    • Susan Hannaford, Club 57 manager
    • Stanley Strachacki, Club 57 founder
    • April Palmieri, Club 57 musician
    • Harvey Wang, Club 57 photographer
  • Pyramid Cocktail Lounge
    • Linda Simpson, Pyramid drag performer
    • Perfidia Kirkham, Pyramid drag performer
    • Ande Whyland, Pyramid photographer
    • Johnny Dynell, Pyramid DJ
  • New St. Marks Baths
    • Jay Blotcher, New St. Marks Baths employee
    • Robert Bryan, New St. Marks Baths patron
    • Susan Tomkin, New St. Marks Baths assistant manager
    • Ira Tattelman, New St. Marks Baths photographer
